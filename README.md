Core aspects of this research project are:
 - Development of a neurosymbolic model (aIchemist) that combines graph-based molecule representations with domain-
specific chemistry knowledge and takes chemical rules into account in training via a “chemical distance loss”.
 - Comparison of several GNN types for precise prediction of chemical reactions with high validity.
 - The invention of a novel family of loss-functions and a compatible Neuro-symbolic architecture.

Uses main datasets from OpenReactionDatabase (ORD).
All Anaconda-Dependencies are captured in package-list.txt,
and all Pip-Dependencies are captured in requirements.txt
